A species of yeast fungi. It is the cause of Pneumocystis pneumonia (PCP). Many people live with this fungus in their lungs everyday and causes little to no problems. However, if your immune system is weakened by HIV/AIDS, cancer, or organ transplants, there is an increased chance of obtaining PCP. Symptoms of PCP include fever, cough, and chest tightness. Can be treated with 2 antibiotics: trimethoprim and sulfamethoxazole.
